How it works

Both the professional and their leader complete the Self Assessment independently and export their PDFs. The Development Plan tool ingests both PDFs, surfaces where scores diverge, and generates a personalised development plan once consensus is reached. All data travels via PDF metadata, no backend, no accounts.
